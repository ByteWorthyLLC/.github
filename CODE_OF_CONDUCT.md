# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
We expect professional, respectful collaboration.

Examples of expected behavior:
- constructive technical feedback
- respectful communication across experience levels
- focus on facts, reproducibility, and user impact

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment, insults, or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information
- abusive or disruptive conduct

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, or restrict access for violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues to: scale@getbyteworthy.com
